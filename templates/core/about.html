About DAW Macros Hub

Empowering music producers through community-driven automation

Our Mission

DAW Macros Hub was created to solve a common problem faced by music producers: the time-consuming process of manually configuring keyboard shortcuts and macros in Cubase. We believe that sharing knowledge and automation tools can significantly enhance productivity and creativity in music production.

Our platform enables producers to share their carefully crafted macros with the community, discover powerful shortcuts created by others, and build a comprehensive library of automation tools that work seamlessly with Steinberg Cubase.

What We Do

Easy Upload

Upload your Cubase Key Commands.xml file and our system automatically parses and extracts all your macros, making them ready to share.

Community Sharing

Share your macros publicly or keep them private. Add descriptions, tags, and categorize them for easy discovery by other producers.

Discover & Download

Browse through thousands of macros created by the community. Search by function, Cubase version, or popularity.

Seamless Integration

Download selected macros as a ready-to-use Key Commands.xml file that you can directly import into your Cubase installation.

Key Features

  • Automatic Parsing: Upload your Key Commands.xml file and we extract all macros automatically
  • Cubase Version Support: Filter macros by your specific Cubase version
  • Rating System: Rate and review macros to help others find the best tools
  • Privacy Control: Keep your macros private or share them with the community
  • User Profiles: Build your reputation as a macro creator and see your contributions
  • Search & Filter: Powerful search tools to find exactly what you need
